About

I am a Cambridge Mathematics graduate. This website contains things such as personal research projects related to ancient history, as well as Cambridge maths lecture notes and content overviews. Last updated 13th July 2025.

Research Projects

  • Governors, Raids and Boats: The šakin māti, the Sealand and Uruk in the context of Neo-Babylonian to Persian continuity (July 2025). 25,000 word project on the roles of officials in Babylonia and the ideological basis of the regime change, mostly analysing administrative documents and aspects of the Nabonidus Chronicle.
  • Artaxerxes II and Anahita: A Discussion of Transformations (October 2024). A methodological evaluation comparing Old Persian, Middle Persian, Avestan and Greek attestations of Anahita to understand the nature of the goddess in different sources.
