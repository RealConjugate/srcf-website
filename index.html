    About This Site

  • I will primarily be using this website for hosting things such as personal research projects related to ancient history that I have worked on, as well as translations of Old Persian inscriptions. I am a Cambridge Mathematics graduate, and also have some maths lecture notes and course content overviews on the site. Last updated 29th December 2025.

    Primary Interests

  • History of the Achaemenid Empire: all aspects, but specifically interested in Persian Babylonia, Lycian monuments and epigraphy, the Persepolis archives, religious history and acculturation, royal inscriptions, and ideology of kingship.
  • Related/adjacent areas of the Ancient Near East or Eastern Mediterranean, especially the Neo-Babylonian Empire, and the history of the post-Bronze Age Levant in general.
  • Study of ancient languages: Old Persian, Akkadian, Imperial Aramaic.

    Research Projects

  • Research project focused on late Neo-Babylonian history (September-December 2025). Work in progress; TBD.
  • Governors, Raids and Boats: The šakin māti, the Sealand and Uruk in the context of Neo-Babylonian to Persian continuity (April-July 2025). 25,000 word project on the roles of officials in Babylonia and the ideological basis of the regime change, mostly analysing administrative documents and aspects of the Nabonidus Chronicle. (Please contact me for access)
  • Artaxerxes II and Anahita: A Discussion of Transformations (October 2024). A methodological evaluation comparing Old Persian, Middle Persian, Avestan and Greek attestations of Anahita to understand the nature of the goddess in different sources.
